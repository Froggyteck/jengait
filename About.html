OUR JOURNEY

Inspiring Young Minds

Founding Vision

Jenga IT Hub was established with a vision to ignite a passion for technology in children. Our founders recognized the growing importance of coding and robotics in the modern world and sought to create a nurturing environment where young learners could explore these fields. Through engaging programs, we aim to bridge the gap between curiosity and knowledge, fostering a love for STEM in our students.

Commitment to Education

We are dedicated to providing high-quality education that is both fun and informative. Our curriculum is designed to cater to various age groups and skill levels, ensuring that every child receives the support they need to thrive. By incorporating hands-on projects and interactive learning methods, we create a dynamic atmosphere that encourages creativity and problem-solving.

Community Impact

At Jenga IT Hub, we believe in giving back to the community. We actively participate in local events and workshops, sharing our knowledge and resources with families and schools. Our aim is not only to educate but also to inspire other organizations to prioritize technology education for children, ensuring that future generations are equipped with the skills they need to succeed.

Our Instructors

Experts in Technology Education

Experienced Educators

Our team comprises experienced educators with extensive backgrounds in coding, robotics, and child development. Each instructor brings a unique set of skills and perspectives, ensuring a well-rounded learning experience for our students.

Passionate Mentors

We pride ourselves on having passionate mentors who are not only knowledgeable in their fields but also dedicated to nurturing young talent. Our instructors take the time to understand each student's needs, helping them grow and develop their skills at their own pace.

Continuous Learning

Our team members are committed to their own professional development, regularly attending workshops and training sessions. This ensures that they are up-to-date with the latest trends in technology and education, allowing them to provide the best possible learning experience for our students.
